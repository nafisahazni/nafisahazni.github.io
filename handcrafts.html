Handcrafted Accessories

Papers & Palettes was established by a Malaysian small business owner, Azzahraa Azni in 2018. It was first started as a collection of paintings and drawings. In 2020, when pandemic hit, the government had announced a restriction movement which bring Azzahraa then ventured into handmade ceramics accessories. Azzahraa has initiated some collection collaboration with Nafisah Azni. For more information, please visit papersandpalettes on Instagram.

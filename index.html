Nafisah Azni is an undergraduate student at University of Wisonsin-Madison. Her major focuses on Economics and Mathematics. While her major employs both qualitative and quantitative methods, she chose to give emphasis more on quantitative methods on discussing economic models. She is also part of the organisation named The International Council of Malaysian Scholars and Associates as the external outreach and publicity director, for North America Chapter. In this organisation, she has been designing posters, liaising with government agencies and companies, and collaborating with Malaysian embassies in the United States and Canada. Besides this professional experience, Nafisah has also developed interest in various path of arts which includes landscape photography, foodtography, and handmade ceramics accessories. She has collaborated with her sister on small project, Papers & Palettes. For more information, please find her contact information on contact page.
